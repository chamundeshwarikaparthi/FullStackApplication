Computer Science Department

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Computer Science Department at George Mason University is a dynamic and thriving academic unit known for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation

The department offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs, catering to students with diverse interests and career goals. Whether students aspire to become software engineers, cybersecurity experts, data scientists, or researchers, the curriculum is designed to provide them with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in their chosen paths.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a commitment to diversity and inclusion, and a strong focus on real-world applications, the Computer Science Department at George Mason University is at the forefront of shaping the future of computer science education and research.
